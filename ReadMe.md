The game is a new version of MasterMind game when the player must identify 4 shapes of diamonds in the right places.
 in each step he get closer to the correct answer by receiving a hints.
1. if he got the shap right he will get a green sord that say he got it on the right position an in the right shap(it does not say where or which is right)
2. a yellow sord if he got it in the right shap but not in the right place.
3. and a red sord if he got both wrong the shap and the position

in addition the player have 3 level which give him less attempt to try to figure the hidden diamonds and time included.
